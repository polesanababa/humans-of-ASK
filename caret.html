My Cares

Part 2

As I mentioned earlier, health is something that I care about. When I was in 6th grade, I started to care about being healthy. I realized that everyone weighed less

than I did and I was ashamed by that. So I started to do exercises and I became good at them. Along with that, I became more thinner and I was happy with the

results. That was until summer when everyone is enjoying rest, electronics, pools, and more. In my case, this made me to gain weight. That was until August and the

Paris Olympics. There is this one Olympian that I look up to and his name is Noah Lyles. He is a track runner, won an event, got a gold medal, and in the first place, qualified for

the Olympics. I became interested in Track and Field and I decided that somehow, I would work to make it to the Olympics. Now even though this is a crazy dream, I

have been putting work in. Also, I know that there is a chance that I won't go to the Olympics, so I have a backup plan (software engineering) I have gained less weight,

been eating healthier foods, and especially, running way more. When I compared my old self and my new self it was extremely different. I went from chubby and

somewhat fast, to ab-popping, vein-popping, leg strong, and extremely fast. So my idea started working out. My only problem is that I had to balance it

out along with school, tutoring, family, and Christianity. Even now I am struggling with that problem especially with finals around the corner. On the other hand, because of

me learning how to balance everything out , I have started to have a vision on where to join a track team next season and how to keep training. So

because of me wanting to become an Olympian, I have been working hard and have been healthier. Also, even my school life hasn't taken too big of a hit. This was my final care.
