My Cares

Part 1

Something that I care about is grades and my physical health. My care

for grades came from my father. Now in my opinion, he is a genius. He

never got failing grades on anything, he skipped masters to a PhD, and

he knows how to deal with my brother. So, when grades actually started

getting included into my life, I tried to follow in my father's steps. See,

the only thing is that school doesn't make it easy to get all As and A+'s.

You actually had to work for it. When I got my first NC on a test, I went

into the school bathroom and cried my heart out. I had never got

anything below a C and on that day, it happened. In my opinion, I failed

and got a 0, even though it was a 65 and she made everyone to redo the test.

I was a failure that couldn't even follow my father's steps when he didn't

even have as close to as much resources as me.

I genuinely wanted to punch myself in the face and leave this realm.

From that day on, I made it my school mission to do as well as possible in school

and not get any NCs , even thought I have messed up twice in the same class

I am not a science whiz, and I am trying my best to be better and not so bad at things.

So, I inherited my care and passion for grades from my father.
