People Understanding Me

Something that I want people to understand about me is

that there is work under the success. What I mean by that

phrase is that people always see my grades and say,

“wow, you are so smart”. The thing is, there was work to

get to the being smart stage. No one ever asks, “How

much work do you put into this class” It seems from my

perspective that people look at me and think that I have

everything under control and everything is easy when it

really isn't! If it was, I wouldn't be working until midnight

on certain things. I would not study excessively. But the

thing is, I work hard and there is work under the success.

Not everything comes easy.
